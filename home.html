About Us

Mission

Our mission is to influence a paradigm shift nationally that redefines the work of student parliaments to give representatives opportunities to influence university decisions whether through advisory or legislative powers. We aim to establish a permanent council, back by a legal foundation, within the organizational structure of the Ministry of Higher Education and all Jordanian universities. We want students to be the centerpiece of our community.

Vision

We want our university to have a distinct identity, blending two cultures and offering equal, fair opportunities in work, applied education, and research for all students.

Our Values

• Education First

• Community Over Hierarchy

• Transparency

• Accountability

• Direct Democracy

• Autonomy

• Reform

• Global Perspective and Collaboration
